About Me
Hello, I'm Amaris Brown
This is my portfolio archive of my latest works. This is my junior year attending Mason Gross School of The Arts. This portfolio consists of works from class projects that I have completed last year.
Art has been an important part of my life. Being a self-taught artist I have gotten inspiration from various places and sources, whether it's just going for a walk, seeing a pattern or art piece that I find intriguing as it helped me form my art pieces. I use a variety of mediums such as graphite, ink, painting, and digital media to create my art pieces.
Most of the graphic work that is shown on this portfolio website were created using Adobe illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign.
